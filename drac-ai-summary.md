## What is Drac AI?

Drac AI is an AI-powered business automation platform that empowers organizations to transform their operations through intelligent chatbots, ad campaign automation, and workflow optimization. The platform is designed to make enterprise-grade AI accessible, affordable, and adaptable for businesses of all sizes — from startups to large enterprises.

At its core, Drac AI removes the technical barriers to adopting artificial intelligence. Businesses can deploy AI-powered customer support in minutes, create professional advertising campaigns with natural language, and automate complex workflows — all without requiring deep technical expertise or massive budgets.

### 2. Markitect — AI-Powered Ad Campaign Creator

Markitect is an AI-driven advertising platform that lets businesses create professional Google Ads and Meta (Facebook/Instagram) campaigns in minutes instead of hours. Users describe their product in natural language, and the AI handles everything — from ad copy generation to audience targeting to one-click publishing.

**Core Capabilities:**

- **Conversational Campaign Creation** — Create campaigns using natural language. The AI guides you step-by-step with smart recommendations and auto-saves progress.
- **Landing Page Analysis** — Paste a URL and the AI automatically extracts product details, generates relevant ad copy, and suggests optimal targeting strategies.
- **Multi-Platform Support** — Create campaigns for Google Ads and Meta (Facebook/Instagram) from a single interface. One campaign, multiple platforms.
- **AI Content Generation** — Generate multiple headline variations, compelling descriptions, and creative recommendations powered by advanced AI.
- **Campaign Management** — Publish with one click, track performance metrics, manage budgets, and target specific audiences and locations.
- **AI Budget Flow** — Smart budget distribution that automatically allocates daily spend across platforms based on real-time performance data. No upfront bulk spending required.

**How It Works (8-Step Flow):**

1. **Enter Your URL** — Paste your landing page URL and let the AI analyze your product or service.
2. **AI Analyzes & Generates** — The AI extracts key information, generates ad copy, and suggests optimal targeting strategies.
3. **Review & Customize** — Review and customize AI-generated headlines, descriptions, and creatives to match your brand.
4. **Select Platforms** — Choose target platforms and set budget and audience preferences.
5. **Publish & Go Live** — One-click publish to Google Ads and Meta platforms simultaneously with optimized settings.
6. **Targeting Optimization** — AI continuously optimizes audience targeting based on demographics, interests, and behaviors.
7. **AI Budget Flow** — Smart budget distribution that automatically allocates spend based on real-time performance.
8. **Performance Reporting** — Track campaign performance with detailed analytics, insights, and AI-driven optimization recommendations.

**Supported Ad Platforms & Formats:**

| Platform | Format |
|----------|--------|
| Google Search | Responsive Search Ads |
| Google Display | Responsive Display Ads |
| Facebook Feed | Image, Video, Carousel |
| Facebook Stories | Image, Video |
| Instagram Feed | Image, Video, Carousel |
| Instagram Stories | Image, Video |
| Instagram Reels | Video Ads |

**Use Cases:**

- **Small Business Advertising** — Perfect for local businesses looking to reach more customers without hiring an agency.
- **E-commerce Campaigns** — Create product campaigns that drive sales with AI-optimized ad copy and targeting.
- **Lead Generation** — Capture high-quality leads with compelling ads and smart audience targeting.
