# Skynift Landing Page Design — "Bloom"

**Date:** 2026-04-03
**Status:** Approved
**Scope:** Cinematic scroll-driven landing page for skynift.com

---

## Brand Hierarchy

- **Skynift** — parent company
  - **Drac AI** — sub-brand (AI-powered business automation platform)
    - **Markitect** — killer product (AI ad campaign creator) — gets centrepiece treatment
    - **SuperBot** — AI chatbot (shares DNA with Helpexa, positioned under Drac)
  - **Helpexa** — direct Skynift product (AI customer service platform)

## Creative Concept: "Bloom"

The entire page is a living garden. Everything unfurls, opens, breathes. Content blossoms rather than loads. Nature is the design language — universally appealing across fine arts, marketing, investors, and all demographics.

The scroll IS the timeline. Each viewport of scroll has purpose.

## Target Audience

Primary: Investors and partners (impress, establish credibility)
Also: Customers, talent, marketing/creative professionals

## Design Language

### Palette

| Role | Color | Hex | Inspiration |
|------|-------|-----|-------------|
| Base | Warm ivory | `#FAF7F2` | Handmade paper |
| Primary text | Deep walnut | `#2C1810` | Rich, warm (not pure black) |
| Accent 1 | Dried rose | `#C4706E` | Warmth without being "pink" |
| Accent 2 | Sage | `#8B9D77` | Growth, life, trust |
| Accent 3 | Turmeric gold | `#D4A843` | Premium, Indian heritage nod |
| Deep accent | Midnight fig | `#2D1B3D` | Depth for Drac AI section |
| Drac gold | Warm gold | `#C9963A` | Drac AI identity |
| Drac amber | Deep amber | `#B8762D` | Markitect warmth |
| Cream | Warm cream | `#F0E8D8` | Markitect section base |

### Typography

- **Headlines:** Cormorant Garant (elegant serif with personality, variable weight)
- **Body:** General Sans (clean, modern, warm humanist sans)
- **Display moments:** Pretext-rendered canvas typography for Scenes 1 and 5a
- **Scale:** Editorial-magazine scale. Headlines at 8-12vw. Type IS the art.

### Motion Philosophy

- Nothing mechanical. Every animation follows organic easing — fast start, gentle settle (like a petal landing)
- Scroll-triggered, but timing feels like breathing
- No bounces, no snaps, no tech-feeling transitions
- Elements ease in like a slow exhale

### Texture

- Subtle grain overlay on base (like fine paper stock)
- No hard borders between sections — colour washes and gradients
- Generous whitespace throughout

## Scroll Journey

**Total scroll depth:** ~12 viewport heights (~12,000px on desktop)

### Scene 1 — "The Seed" (0–200vh)

**Type:** Sticky canvas with scroll-mapped animation
**Palette:** Warm ivory base

The viewport opens nearly empty — a single small organic shape in the dead centre, like a droplet of watercolour on paper.

As the user scrolls, the shape unfurls. Petals of colour (dried rose, sage, whisper of gold) expand outward in slow organic motion. Asymmetric, like an actual flower opening in time-lapse. Each petal is a soft gradient blob rendered on canvas.

At ~60% scroll progress, the Skynift wordmark emerges from the centre — rendered via pretext directly onto the canvas, so the type IS the flower. Large Cormorant Garant serif. Below it in small General Sans: "Intelligence that grows with you."

At 100%, the bloom settles. Canvas unpins. Clear space begins.

**Feel:** Slow, meditative, awe. "These people have taste."

### Scene 2 — "The Deepening" (200–300vh)

**Type:** Sticky full-viewport colour transition
**Palette:** Ivory darkening to midnight fig `#2D1B3D`

The warm ivory background begins deepening. Sage and rose tones transform into gold and amber. Like a garden transitioning from day to golden hour.

During the sticky section, a single line of text fades in at centre viewport, large serif:

> "Now, meet the engine."

The text itself shifts from walnut brown to warm gold as the background deepens. When the transition completes, the sticky section releases.

**Feel:** Dramatic. Cinematic. The audience leans forward.

### Scene 3 — "Drac AI Arrives" (300–400vh)

**Type:** Scroll-triggered sequential reveals
**Palette:** Midnight fig + warm gold

On the deep fig background, the Drac AI identity materialises.

The word **"drac"** appears letter by letter — each letter triggers at a specific scroll position. Bold, large (12vw), warm gold against dark. Below: "AI-powered business intelligence" in small cream sans-serif.

Below the wordmark, two paths diverge — a subtle SVG branch line draws itself. One path goes left (SuperBot), one continues rightward and downward for much longer (Markitect). The visual weight signals where the story heads.

**Feel:** Arrival. Authority. Anticipation.

### Scene 4 — "Markitect: The Crown Bloom" (400–750vh)

This is the centrepiece. Three+ viewport-heights of scroll-driven storytelling.

#### 4a — The Reveal (400–500vh)

**Type:** Sticky canvas
**Palette:** Fig background → bold geometric bloom in gold and coral

A new bloom opens — bolder, more geometric than the opening. Gold and coral on dark. More confident, more powerful.

Inside the bloom, "Markitect" renders in massive display serif via pretext on canvas. Below: "Describe your product. Launch your campaign. AI handles the rest."

The bloom completes. The dark background washes away to reveal warm cream beneath — like curtains parting.

#### 4b — The 8-Step Flow (500–650vh)

**Type:** Horizontal scroll (vertical scroll pinned, content moves horizontally)
**Palette:** Cream → soft gold → warm amber (continuous gradient across 8 panels)

The viewport pins. Vertical scroll maps to horizontal movement. Eight full-viewport-width panels:

1. "Paste your URL" — elegant URL input illustration
2. "AI analyses everything" — abstract lines connecting, forming a map
3. "Headlines generated" — three headline options appearing, staggered
4. "Choose your platforms" — Google + Meta icons, softly illuminated
5. "One click to publish" — a button that glows as you reach it
6. "Smart targeting" — audience segments as gentle concentric rings
7. "AI Budget Flow" — flowing river of gold (animated particles on canvas)
8. "Watch it perform" — minimal dashboard with counting numbers

Transitions between panels: smooth cross-fade. Horizontal movement mapped 1:1 to vertical scroll — direct and tactile.

#### 4c — The Impact (650–750vh)

**Type:** Scroll-triggered count-up animations
**Palette:** Warm amber

Three massive numbers stack vertically, each triggered as it crosses viewport centre:

- **"70%"** — support tickets eliminated
- **"5 min"** — from zero to live campaign
- **"$0.01"** — per conversation overflow

Numbers in massive serif (20vw). Labels in small sans below. Scale of type IS the design.

**Feel of entire Scene 4:** The part people screenshot. Horizontal scroll feels physical. 8-step flow makes complexity feel inevitable. Impact numbers land hard after the story.

### Scene 5 — "SuperBot: The Companion Bloom" (750–850vh)

**Type:** Single sticky viewport
**Palette:** Warm cream + deep indigo accents (Drac AI identity, distinct from Helpexa)

- Left half: large serif — "Your AI that actually knows your business"
- Right half: a chat conversation animates line by line as you scroll. Customer asks, SuperBot responds with context-aware accuracy. Messages timed to reading pace.

**Feel:** Competent, clear, trustworthy. Strong supporting act.

### Scene 6 — "Helpexa: A Petal of Its Own" (850–950vh)

**Type:** Parallax floating card
**Palette:** Returns toward warm ivory with soft rose and sage

A single elegant card scrolls into view with gentle parallax (0.85x scroll speed — floats).

Content:
- "Helpexa" in serif
- One line: "AI support that understands your business in minutes."
- Three feature words stacked: **Train. Embed. Launch.** — each fades in sequentially with scroll, 50ms stagger
- A small, curated chat widget illustration (editorial, not live demo)

One card. One petal. Established, polished, live. The card parallaxes upward and fades.

**Feel:** Brief, confident. Like one look walking a fashion runway.

### Scene 7 — "The Garden View" (950–1050vh)

**Type:** SVG/canvas illustration that draws itself on scroll
**Palette:** Warm ivory

All products visible as botanical illustrations in organic composition:
- Markitect: largest, most elaborate flower
- SuperBot: medium bud
- Helpexa: delicate bloom

Connected by stems and leaves. Draws itself as you scroll.

Trust signals appear around the illustration:
- "SOC 2 certified infrastructure"
- "AES-256 encryption"
- "50+ languages"
- "99.9% uptime"

Each with subtle fade + upward drift, staggered.

**Feel:** Cohesion. "This is an ecosystem, not a collection of tools."

### Scene 8 — "The Invitation" (1050–1200vh)

**Type:** Static with subtle animation
**Palette:** Warm golden ivory

Large serif, centred: "Let's grow something together."

Three paths:
- **"Try Markitect"** — primary CTA, warm gold button, slightly larger
- **"Explore Helpexa"** — secondary, outlined
- **"Talk to us"** — tertiary, text link with arrow

Contact: contact@skynift.com
Small Skynift wordmark.

Background: faintest echo of the opening bloom — a ghosted watercolour shape, nearly invisible. Page ends where it began.

**Feel:** Warm, personal, unhurried. An invitation to a garden.

## Technical Architecture

### Stack

| Layer | Choice | Reason |
|-------|--------|--------|
| Framework | Next.js (App Router) | Routing for future pages, SSR for SEO, component model |
| Animation | GSAP + ScrollTrigger | Industry standard for scroll-driven animation, pin/scrub |
| Smooth scroll | Lenis | Normalises scroll, buttery feel |
| Canvas text | @chenglou/pretext | DOM-free typography for canvas scenes |
| Canvas rendering | HTML5 Canvas API | Bloom animations, particle effects |
| SVG animation | GSAP DrawSVG or CSS stroke-dashoffset | Branch lines, garden illustration |
| Styling | Tailwind CSS + CSS custom properties | Utility-first with dynamic palette transitions |
| Deployment | Vercel (or Cloudflare) | Edge, fast, Next.js native |

### Scroll Technique Map

| Technique | Scenes | Implementation |
|-----------|--------|---------------|
| Sticky canvas + scroll-mapped animation | 1, 2, 4a | GSAP ScrollTrigger pin + scrub, Canvas API + pretext |
| Parallax floating elements | 6 (Helpexa card) | GSAP ScrollTrigger with speed offset |
| Scroll-triggered sequential reveals | 3 (letter-by-letter), 7 (trust signals) | GSAP ScrollTrigger with stagger |
| Horizontal scroll (pin + scrub) | 4b (Markitect 8-step) | GSAP ScrollTrigger horizontal pin |
| Scroll-triggered count-up | 4c (impact numbers) | GSAP ScrollTrigger + custom counter |
| SVG path draw-on-scroll | 3 (branch), 7 (garden) | GSAP/CSS stroke-dashoffset |
| Background colour transitions | 2, 4b | GSAP ScrollTrigger + CSS custom properties |
| Smooth scroll normalisation | Entire page | Lenis |
| Canvas typography | 1, 4a | pretext library |

### Performance Requirements

- 60fps scroll on modern devices
- requestAnimationFrame for all canvas updates
- Lazy-load canvas scenes (only initialise when approaching viewport)
- Throttle scroll listeners via Lenis (not raw scroll events)
- Preload critical fonts (Cormorant Garant, General Sans)
- Image/asset lazy loading for editorial illustrations
- Total JS bundle target: <150KB gzipped (excl. GSAP)

### Responsive Strategy

- Desktop: full cinematic experience as described
- Tablet: simplified parallax, horizontal scroll preserved
- Mobile: sticky sections still work, horizontal scroll → vertical cards, canvas animations simplified (fewer particles, simpler bloom)
